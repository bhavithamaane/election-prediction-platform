Karnataka Historical Milestones

Key statistics of previous Karnataka Lok Sabha elections

2024

2024 Karnataka Lok Sabha

BJP won 17 of 28 seats in Karnataka with 46.1% vote share. INC won 9 seats with 45.4% vote share. JD(S) won 2 seats.

2019

2019 Karnataka Lok Sabha

BJP dominated Karnataka winning 25 of 28 seats with 51.4% vote share. INC won 1 seat and JD(S) won 1 seat.

2014

2014 Karnataka Lok Sabha

BJP won 17 seats in Karnataka. INC won 9 seats. JD(S) won 2 seats. The state saw a closely contested battle between BJP and INC.
